About

Youngstrive Rides is a transit/party bus service located in Nassau, Bahamas. We offer a variety of packages to meet your needs, no matter the size of your party or event. From birthdays and anniversaries to corporate events and weddings, we can help you create the perfect atmosphere for your event. Our experienced staff will work with you to customize your party bus and create the perfect environment for your special occasion.

We strive to provide a safe, enjoyable experience for all of our customers. Our buses are equipped with state-of-the-art safety features and our drivers are licensed and insured. We go the extra mile to ensure that your event is worry-free.
